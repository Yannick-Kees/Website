Master of Science: Mathematics

I am delighted to mention my Master of Science in Mathematics degree from Rheinische Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität Bonn in this section of my CV. I pursued this degree from October 2020 to November 2022 and obtained an excellent grade of 1.1. Completing my thesis, 'Learning geometric phase field representations,' under the guidance of Prof. Dr. M. Rumpf & Prof. Dr. T. Laux was a significant achievement for me. Additionally, pursuing a minor in Informatik allowed me to explore my interest in computer science and broaden my knowledge. Along with that, I gained analytical skills and mastered the use of Deep Learning methods which are highly sought-after skills in the field of mathematics and technology. Overall, this section of my CV represents my proficiency in mathematics and computer science and showcases my ability to use analytical tools to solve complex problems.
